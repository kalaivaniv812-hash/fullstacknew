The Office Romance

​The Beginning While Kannmani was still in college, she received a job offer. Just two days after finishing her exams, she started her career. After three months of training, she moved into her actual workspace in the fourth month. She made many new colleagues and was learning a lot; everything was going well. ​The First Encounter One day, as she was sitting at her desk, the door opposite her opened. A guy walked in. The moment Kannmani saw him, she thought, "Who is this? He looks so unique." She felt an instant connection and liked him immediately. However, they couldn't see each other daily because he worked on the 2nd floor and she was on the 3rd. ​The Growing Attraction

Some time passed without them seeing each other. Then, one day, he came up to the 3rd floor to visit a friend. Kannmani was overjoyed to see him after so many days. While she had been noticing him all this time, he had no idea who she was or that she was interested. It was a strong attraction on her part, but she didn't ask anyone about him at first. ​The "Investigation" Eventually, she couldn't help herself and asked her Team Lead (TL) about him. The TL told her that the guy worked on the 2nd floor and asked, "Why are you asking?" Kannmani casually replied, "No reason, sir, he just looks quite different." ​She later told her friend that she liked his look. Her friend started teasing her whenever he passed by, the friend would loudly say, "Look, your guy is going by!" One day, he actually overheard this. He went and told his friend what he heard. Coincidentally, his friend knew Kannmani and told him, "That girl likes you, but it’s not love yet." He was shocked to hear this. ​Harry Potter Kannmani eventually learned his name: Harry. Interestingly, he lived up to the name wearing glasses and looking very much like Harry Potter. They started noticing each other and sharing occasional glances. ​The Turning Point After a few months, Harry didn't show up to the office for six days. Some said he was unwell, while others rumored he had quit. Worried, Kannmani found him on Instagram and sent a friend request. She messaged him asking about his health. ​Harry saw the message three hours later and asked, "Who are you?" Kannmani replied, "I see you daily. I'm the one who said you have a very unique look." Harry realized who she was, and they began talking. ​From Friendship to Forever

When Harry returned to the office, Kannmani met him in person to check on his health. From there, they started spending their tea breaks and lunch hours together. They grew close and truly understood one another. ​After a few months, Harry realized his feelings for her. He shared his heart with her, saying, "I really like you. I feel like we should spend our lives together. Will you marry me?" ​Kannmani was bursting with happiness. She had started with a crush and now the person she liked was proposing. He told her, "Take your time and let me know." That night, she was too happy to sleep. ​The Proposal The next morning, they met. Harry smiled at her, and she told him she wanted to talk. She asked him to meet her at a Church in the evening. There, after praying, she shared her feelings and said, "Yes." She told him that her love would belong only to him for the rest of her life. ​Today They have been happily together for four and a half years now, continuing their beautiful journey of love
